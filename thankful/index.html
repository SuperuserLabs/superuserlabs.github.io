Be Thankful

Thankful is a browser plugin that helps you donate to everyone you visit online using cryptocurrency.

Subscribe to updates!

Support everyone you like

Thankful tries to make it as easy as possible to reward every creator you like online.

Zero transaction fees*

The only fees you pay are cryptocurrency fees, and the completely optional "Support the development of Thankful"-fee.

A good usecase for cryptocurrency

Direct-to-creator donations would never be possible without cryptocurrency like Ethereum or Bitcoin, but now that they are we hope they will become the norm.
